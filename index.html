About Us

The Malaria Analysis Project is dedicated to creating a comprehensive resource for malaria data in India, with the ultimate goal of reducing malaria cases nationwide. We provide valuable insights and detailed analysis to support policy-makers, health organizations, and communities in their fight against the disease.


Our efforts include extensive data collection, mapping malaria-prone areas, and offering advanced analytics. By analyzing malaria outbreaks across the country, we empower governments and healthcare organizations with the information they need to implement effective strategies and interventions. Through our work, we aim to contribute significantly to the ongoing battle against malaria in India.
