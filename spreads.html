How Malaria Spreads

How Malaria is Contracted

Don’t let malaria be a threat—learn how to protect yourself and your community from this deadly disease.

  • Mosquito Bites

    Preventable and treatable, malaria is spread through the bite of infected Anopheles mosquitoes. Protecting against bites is essential to stopping the disease.

  • Vulnerable Populations

    Infants, young children, pregnant women, and immunocompromised individuals are most at risk for severe malaria, making prevention and early detection critical.

  • Parasite Transmission

    The malaria-causing parasite (Plasmodium) enters the bloodstream through mosquito bites, with P. falciparum and P. vivax posing the greatest threat in India.
